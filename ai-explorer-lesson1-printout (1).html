Today's Big Idea: AI recognizes patterns. Humans understand meaning.

✂️
Step 1 — Choose Your Rule

The Algorithm picks one rule secretly. Start with an easy one, then try a tricky one in Round 2!

🌱 Things that are ALIVE
🍳 Found in a KITCHEN
✈️ Things that can FLY
📚 Used at SCHOOL
🌊 Found near WATER
🟡 Things that are YELLOW
🔤 Has the letter "A" in its name
🔋 Needs ELECTRICITY

✂️ Cut out · Fold · Pick one secretly for each round

📊
Step 2 — Record Your Training Data

The Trainer records every guess and response. This is your training data — look for the pattern!

Object named In or Out? Updated hypothesis (what's my rule?)
🧭

"AI recognizes patterns. Humans understand meaning."

This one sentence explains almost all of modern AI.

✏️
Reflect + Write
💬
Talk About It
📱 What apps or tech do you use that might be using AI? How would it have learned to do what it does?
🤖 If AI can only learn from examples, what happens if the examples are wrong or unfair?
🧠 What's something you understand that would be nearly impossible to teach AI just from examples?
